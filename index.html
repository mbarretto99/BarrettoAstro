About

Hi, I'm Mark

I'm an astrophotographer and maker based in Perth, Western Australia. Barretto Astro began the way most good projects do — I couldn't find the exact part I needed for my own rig, so I designed and 3D-printed it myself.

Everything here is printed in ABS: tough, impact-resistant, and — crucially — UV-resistant, so it won't crack or fade after long sessions under the sun and stars. Each piece is designed by an astrophotographer, for astrophotographers.

If you don't see your camera, scope or setup, just message me — custom sizes and one-off designs are my favourite thing to make.
